KIMS BLOOD DONOR CLUB™

Celebrating lives!
B - Beautiful
L - Life
O - Only
O - On
D - Donating
The Kims Blood Donor App puts the power to save lives in your fingertips.
- A new of its kind initiative by students of Karpaga Vinayaga Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre to save and uplift lives.
- As a group of young motivated students, we strive to bridge the gap between those in need and those are willing to satisfy the need.
- We simplify the process of Blood Donation, taking into consideration the compliance of the donor and making it as easy as possible for the recipient to receive blood.
- We want to maximize the number of donors and minimize the number of lives lost due to suffering from the lack of donors.
So, let us all unite in celebrating lives.
Main Features:
✔ Simple and clean user interface makes it easy to use.
✔ Request Blood and find your Donors of needed blood group and through location immediately.
✔Keep a track of blood donations of your request.
✔ Get a push notification/SMS of your Request.
✔ Get latest update for blood request.
✔ Full Editable Profile of Donor.
✔ Get notify when you are able to donate blood.
✔ Keep in touch with all kind academic and awareness campaigns in areas of our services.
✔ For emergency patient anyone can contact with the local organization through phone call.
✔ Know All important facts to donate blood.
✔ Contact anytime to coordinators in case of emergency or any query.
